Financial Inclusion

Home / Financial Inclusion / Insurance

Categories

    Various categories of insurance services managed by CSC SPV are as follows:

    Life Insurance

    Insurance policy promises to pay a pre-decided amount in case of death of the insured person or in case of survival of the insured person up to a pre-decided time period.
    Life Insurance policy is a long-term contract and needs to be renewed by continuous payment of Premium, called Renewal Premium, to keep the policy in force.

    General Insurance

    These are typically short-term contracts of 1 year. Several products which are included under general insurance are:
    i. Motor Insurance- Motor insurance is governed by two ways:

  • a. Motor comprehensive
  • b. Motor Third Party Insurance

    • ii. Fire/property insurance
    iii. Travel Insurance
    iv. Personal accident insurance

    Health Insurance

    Health Insurance policies are typically short term (1 year) contracts and cover medical expenses incurred by insured in case of hospitalization due to unforeseen circumstances.

    Crop Insurance

    It acts as a safeguard for farmer income and indemnifies crop loss due to uncertain weather conditions.
    The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a flagship scheme launched by the Government of India in 2016 on the theme of One-Nation-One-Scheme. The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) and Ministry of Agriculture authorized CSC SPV to enroll farmers across India under PMFBY.
