Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is the eligibility criteria?

Eligibility criteria required for enrolling in the course:
• Disability Type/Category: Orthopedic/Loco motor and Speech & Hearing Impaired only
• Minimum 40% disability with valid disability certificate
• Applicant must not have undergone any other skill training course sponsored by Govt. of India during the period of two years from the last date of receipt of application.
• Age limit: 16 years to 59 years
• Qualification: As per job role

How targets will be allocated?

Targets will be allocated on the basis of survey conducted and training infrastructure availability at centre.

What is the minimum attendance required by candidate for successfully passing the course?

Minimum attendance require to pass the course is 80% through biometric attendance system.

What is the remuneration cost given to the candidate?

The candidates will get Rs. 1000 per month (as per course duration as Conveyance allowance) and Rs. 5000/- per beneficiary as Assistive Aids cost through AEPS (This is only for successfully passes candidates).

How will VLEs get their payments/commission?

VLEs will get their commission only for passed candidates in their bank account i.e. registered with CSC.

What documents are needed to be collected from candidates?

Documents which must be collected from candidates while registering for the course are:
• 2 photographs
• Disability Certificate Copy
• Aadhaar Card Copy
• Bank Details/Passbook/Statement Coopy
• Educational Certificates Copy
• Undertaking Form

What are the roles and responsibilities of VLE?

Various significant roles and responsibilities of VLEs are as follows:
• Self Assessment (validation) of Skill centre
• Mobilization & selection of PwD candidates as per their eligibility criteria
• Registration of eligible PwD candidates on CSC Skill Portal
• Registration of candidates on Biometric Attendance System
• Training of registered candidates
• Assessment of candidates
• Distribution of certificates to eligible candidates
• Provide support to passed candidates for employment/self employment through Job Outreach activities

What is the minimum infrastructure requirement for a center to start PwD batch?

Minimum infrastructure requirement for conducting PwD training session are:
• Capacity of class room & lab both
• Availability of placement and entrepreneurship cell
• Type of construction of centre's building
• Availability of biometric attendance system
• Availability of CCTV camera/video conferencing
• Availability of internet connectivity
• Availability of power backup
• Availability of printer, photocopier and scanner
• Availability of separate wash room facility for male and female candidates
• Availability of safe and clean drinking water
• Availability of first-aid kit
• Availability of fire-fighting equipment
• Availability of ramps/lift
• Availability of sufficient furniture
• Qualified trainer
• Availability of computers/laptops
