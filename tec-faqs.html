Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is Telecentre Entrepreneur Courses (TEC)?

Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC) is a certification course designed by CSC Academy. This course is for all the Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLE) who intends to become a certified Entrepreneur.

What is the main objective of the programme?

Main objective of the Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC) is to aware the Village level Entrepreneur (VLE) about the various government services provided by Common Services Centre and to make them certified under Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC).

What is the eligibility criterion for Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC)?

All the CSC VLEs can become a certified VLE under Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC).

What is the fee of Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC)?

The fee of Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC) is Rs 500/- + GST (non-refundable).

What is the benefit of the program towards the VLE?

After studying the Telecentre Entrepreneur course (TEC) and clearing the online assessment(s) successfully, VLE will be certified under Telecentre Entrepreneur course (TEC).

What is the duration of the training program?

The duration of the training program is 40 hours.

What are the modules which are covered under the Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC)?

Total 10 Modules are covered under Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC):
Module 1: Fundamentals of Telecentre
Module 2: Entrepreneur & Entrepreneurship
Module 3: G2C & B2C Services
Module 4: Common Services Centre Scheme
Module 5: Digital Seva Portal
Module 6: Digital Securities and Wellness
Module 7: Finance and Accounting
Module 8: Goods and Services Tax (GST)
Module 9: Soft Skills
Module 10: Business Communication

Is it mandatory to clear all the Assessments to get certified?

Yes, it is mandatory to clear all the 10 Assessments to get certified under Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC).

How can a VLE get their certificate under Telecentre Entrepreneur Course (TEC)?

After clearing all the Assessments successfully, VLE can download the certificate online through TEC portal.
