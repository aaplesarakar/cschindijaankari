Process Flow

SBI FASTag

How to purchase SBI FASTag

Step 1: Login to Digital Seval Portal through respective User ID and password
Step 2: Search for SBI FASTag and click on SBI FASTag option
Step 3: VLE will be directed to window of FASTag, and then click on Registration option
Step 4: Fill all accurate information under the service registration form and click on Submit
Step 5: Then click on Tag Purchase and select bank name along with vehicle class and then click on Submit option
Step 6: VLE will be directed to CSC Wallet for payment and enter correct Password. Then click on Validate option to proceed with payment of tags
Step 7: Click on SBI Limit option to set recharge amount limit for Top-up
Step 8: Click on History option to check the Tag status
Step 9: Click on Receiving option and further click on Confirm button in the action tab.

Registration

Step 1: Under the Recharge and Issue option, select bank name as SBI
Step 2: Login window will appear and login into the system by entering registered mobile number and password
Step 3: VLE will be directed to dashboard of SBI FASTag services https://fastag.onlinesbi.com/AGENTADMIN/login
Step 4: Click on Customer Registration option and further select on customer registration option for individual registration
Step 5: Select individual option and fill all correct details of customer along with vehicle class information in the registration form
Step 6: Enter details pertaining to Tag ID mapping and click on Register Customer
Step 7: For registration of multiple tags i.e. registration for customer having more than 1 vehicle, click on Customer Registration option and further select Multiple Tags Registration option
Step 8: Enter mobile number for registration of multiple tags
Step 9: Enter the desired number of required tags and click on Submit option
Step 10: Click on Customer Registration option and further select customer registration option
Step 11: Select corporate option and fill all correct details along with vehicle class information in the registration form. Also verify the mobile number and then click on Submit option
Step 12: VLE will fill the required information and send the same to fastag@csc.gov.in and CSC will upload the details after which VLE will click on Corporate Registration option
Step 13: Enter the registered mobile number and vehicle number
Step 14: Then enter the barcode
Step 15: Under the Corporate Registration option select Corporate Management and select suitable information pertaining to top-up, payment, etc.

Tag Recharge

Step 1: To recharge SBI FASTags click on Tag Recharge option and enter customer’s mobile number
Step 2: Customer’s vehicle information will be shown and click on Recharge option against the vehicle
Step 3: Enter the recharge amount and click on Submit option.

HDFC FASTag

How to purchase HDFC FASTag

Step 2: Search for HDFC FASTag and click on HDFC FASTag option
Step 3: VLE will be directed to window of FASTag, and then click on Registration option
Step 4: Fill all accurate information under the service registration form and click on Submit
Step 5: Then click on Tag Purchase and select bank name along with vehicle class and then click on Submit option
Step 6: VLE will be directed to CSC Wallet for payment and enter correct Password. Then click on Validate option to proceed with payment of tags

Registration

Step 1: Under the Recharge and Issue option, select bank name as HDFC
Step 2: Login window will appear and click on Login with Digital Seva Connect to login into the system
Step 3: VLE will be directed to dashboard of HDFC FASTag window. Under that click on New Creation
Step 4: Enter customer mobile number and then enter the OTP
Step 5: Fill all the demographic and vehicle details under the registration form
Step 6: Upload all required documents and select product. Then click on Proceed to Payment

Tag Recharge

Step 1: To recharge HDFC FASTags click on Recharge option under HDFC FASTag dashboard
Step 2: Enter the registered mobile number and select the customer ID
Step 3: Then enter the recharge amount and click on Submit option.

Step 3: Validate PAN details and click on Validate PAN
Step 4: Fill all relevant details under the Customer Registration form and then click on Register User
