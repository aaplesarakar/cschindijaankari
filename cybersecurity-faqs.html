Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is the benefit of the course?

The course gives:
• Fundamental knowledge and practical abilities in Cybersecurity
• Detect attack methodology
• Combat hackers from intrusion or other suspicious attempts at connection to gain unauthorized access to a computer and its resources
• Protect data and respond to threats that occur over the Internet.

What are learning outcomes for students through this course?

A student who completes the course will be able to:
• Follow a structured model in Security Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC).
• Detect attack methodology and combat hackers from intrusion or other suspicious attempts at connection to gain unauthorized access to a computer and its resources.
• Protect data and respond to threats that occur over the Internet.
• Plan, implement and audit operating systems' security in a networked, multi-platform and cross platform environment.
• Provide contingency operations that include administrative planning process for incident response, disaster recovery, and business continuity planning within information security.

What is the course duration?

The duration of the course is 20 days that includes 30 hours of online training and assessment which takes place in 5 days a week i.e. 1.5 hours per day.

Does this course fall under government scheme?

No, this course is not under government scheme. Candidate has to pay all fees.
VLE will get his/her commission at time of payment which is included in course fee.

Who is this course for?

The course is designed for beginners where basic mathematics knowledge is required. School kids, engineers, designers, and anyone looking to learn artificial intelligence, machine learning, deep learning & programming will be benefitted through this course. Also, all those who want to enhance their skill set and find a potential business & job opportunity in the space of Cyber security, IT, Programming can opt for the course.

What is the course fee?

Course fee is Rs 1,180/- + GST per candidate registration.

What are the course modules

The course modules are:
• Cyber space
• Cyber Security
• Password
• Endpoint Security
Calculations - Computation - Communication
Essentials of Cyber Security Concept
• Cyber Security and Information Security
• Fundamentals of Information Assurance
• Concept of CIA Triad
Functioning of Internet and Network Layers
• Internet Protocol Address
• Domain Name System
• Actions on the Internet after Clicking a Link
• Layered Architecture of Networks
Basic Threats and Vulnerabilities Part -I
• Threats through Emails
• Malicious Codes or Malware Threats
• Threat Classification
Basic Threats and Vulnerabilities Part -II
• Virus
• Virus Grouping
Basic Threats and Vulnerabilities Part - III
• Worms
• Classification of Worms
• Trojan
Additional Threats and Vulnerabilities
• Spyware
• Botnets
Exploitation: Threats and Vulnerabilities
• SQL Injections
• Buffer Overflow
• Cross Site Scripting
New Trends in Cyber Threats and Attacks
• Actors on the Scene
• Possible Targets
• Advanced Persistent Threat
• Blended Threats
• DoS (Denial of Service) Attack
• Ransomware
• Insider Threats and Attacks

What is the commission for VLE?

VLE may charge as per the local market value from the candidate.

When does a candidate get their certificate?

After completion of the online course, CSC Academy and Ecole Supérieure Robert de Sorbon®️ Institute certificate will be awarded to participants followed by an online assessment.

Is trainer required for these courses?

This course is based on self learning approach, so there is no such requirement of trainer. But if there is batch of candidates for the course so it is advised to arrange a trainer for better understanding.

What is the learning mode for students?

The course is based on an online learning approach where the real time environment has been created for the better understanding. Digital content allow candidate to learn candidate anytime, anywhere.

What are credentials of student/candidate?

Login credentials are available at payment receipt. Candidates can change their credentials after first login

How much candidates are required for a batch?

There is no such limitation in these courses. But it is advised to have 10 students in a batch.

What is the examination process?

An online examination is conducted after the course completion for assessment of students. Examination consists of multiple choice questions (MCQs) and passing percentage in the examination is 50% for all modules.
