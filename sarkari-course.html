Sarkari Pariksha

It is an online test preparation platform that is available on web and mobile formats. The service provides test prep for several Central and State level exams in Hindi and English languages. The portal provides Tutorials, Practice questions, Full and Subject Mock tests with detailed analysis and feedback.

CSC SPV collaborated with Sarkari Pariksha platform with a vision to enhance online competitive examination skill in rural students and transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The programme envisages to enhance the basic skill of Quantitative Aptitude (Mathematical Ability), General Awareness, English Comprehension (English Language Learning), Reasoning (Verbal and Non Verbal), Other Subjects like Social Sciences and Sciences of students.

Course Features

• Information: The service provides precise information about eligibility of the posts, patterns of the exams and syllabus of the leading exams. It also provides latest trends of the exams, e.g. Paper, Subject, Module wise break-up of the leading exams.
• Preparation: Program covering 60 Exams of both Central and State level with a huge 80,000+ question bank for Practice questions & Mock test papers.
• Deep Analysis: Multiple Reports – Subject wise, Sectional, Topic level and Time Reports. The service also provides State and National Rank among its users.
• Vernacular focus: As of now test prep for over 60 exams which are being provided in Hindi, and English languages. Work is on to extend it to other regional languages.
