Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is Vedic Maths Course?

Vedic Math is one of the most refined and efficient system of Mathematics. It is a collection of 29 techniques/sutras to perform mathematical operations. With the help of Vedic Math, one can perform the mathematical calculations 10-15 times faster than the conventional methods.

What is the main objective of the course?

This course is designed carefully in such a manner which helps students to learn and perform the calculations at much faster rate with 100% accuracy. Vedic Math is useful for students, teachers and those who are preparing for competitive exams as well. It has become important now to make students learn Vedic Math concepts at an early age to create a strong foundation of Mathematics.

Who all can join the course?

Any school students (class 6 onwards) or college student or aspirants of competitive exams or teachers can join this course.

What is the syllabus of the course?

The course includes:
1. Introduction to Vedic Math
2. Addition (4 concepts)
3. Subtraction (5 concepts)
4. Multiplication (8 concepts)
5. Division (3 concepts)
6. Digital Root
7. Verification of answers

What is the medium of instructions for the course?

The course is conducted in English or bilingual language.

What is the mode of course delivery?

The course is conducted online through live classes via Webex.

What is the course duration?

The course duration is 45 days and each class is conducted for 1 hour.

What is the frequency of classes?

Thrice in a week (Monday, Thursday, Friday) classes are organized.

How many students can be enrolled in a batch?

50 students can be enrolled in a batch

How will the student get information about the commencement of course?

Students will be intimated through WhatsApp or SMS about the course commencement information. Also, timing and course link will be shared to the registered mobile numbers one day in advance.

Does student gets hand-on approach in the course?

Every live class is followed by live quiz. The quiz helps in assessment of knowledge attain by the student through the course.

What is the passing percentage for a student?

Minimum qualifying score per quiz is 60% for a student.

How will the student attain his/her certificate?

Certificate of course completion is provided by CSC Academy. It is provided to those students, who score minimum 60% in each quiz.

What is the course fee?

The course fee is Rs 300.
