Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What does a student attain through Toppr course?

At Toppr, approach of studying is divided into 4 parts:
 Learn: The section gives student hundreds of hours of video classes, concept notes and study material.
 Practice: This section adapts to progress and gives the right questions, sets and adaptive tests to practice.
 Doubt: In this section student can and a tutor will respond to you instantly. Student can even upload a picture of your question.
 Tests: The section gives real exam practice and let student compete with lakhs of students from around the country.

If a student is already enrolled into a coaching class, why should he/she use Toppr?

Whether a student uses coaching classes or other ways to supplement school teaching, a large part of learning is self study. It helps in making the most of their limited time. Student can learn or practice anytime, anywhere at your own pace; ask questions 24x7 and take tests with lakhs of students. Toppr provides guidance like a personal tutor. Toppr learns about the student, adapts to their needs and grows with them to help in achieving their study goals.

What kind of study material student gets through Toppr?

Toppr has a question bank that has more than 2 lakh questions, with hints, solutions. Toppr has more than 500 hours of video lectures and concept notes. These are neatly organised into chapters and topics. Also depending on student’s progress Toppr shows exactly what is needed to enhance the learning.

What is the Basic Plan of Toppr?

The Basic Plan includes Online Classes module . This plan will help student start learning better with video classes that explain complex concepts easily. Student can also interact with tutors during live classes or just focus on the highlights of each chapter with Stories.

What is the Advanced Plan of Toppr?

The Advanced Plan contains all the features of the Basic Plan, while additionally offering the Adaptive Practice module. The adaptive algorithms evaluate student’s performance and suggest questions that will help to master every topic. Every question is personalized to assist in learn better.

What is the Ultimate Plan of Toppr?

The Ultimate Plan is a comprehensive learning solution that includes all modules:
Online Classes
Adaptive Practice
Mock Tests
Live Doubts
They’re designed to complete full circle of learning. It includes exclusive feature i.e. Live Doubts. With it, student can ask any doubt to experts who are available 24x7 to solve it.

What is the Online Classes module of Toppr?

The Online Classes module includes: Live Classes, Video Classes, Concepts and Stories . Students can learn at their own pace with short and easy online video classes as well as live classes. Revise definitions, diagrams and more at one glance with concepts. Learn any topic visually in minutes through stories.

What is the Mock Tests module of Toppr?

The Mock Tests module contains 10 test series with more than 40 part or full tests in each. Student also gets to solve previous years’ papers for major exams. All of them come with comprehensive performance reports that helps student to improve. A student can also create their own tests by selecting duration and topics of your choice.

What is the Adaptive Practice module of Toppr?

Under the Adaptive Practice module student can practice over 3.5 lakh questions that come with hints and solutions. Platform’s adaptive algorithms evaluate their performance and suggest questions that will help in mastering every topic. Every question is personalised to help a student to learn better.

What is the Live Doubts module of Toppr?

The Live Doubts is a 24x7 facility, also called as "Doubts on Chat". This module allows a student to or doubt with a community of experts who are available 24x7. Student can either type the question or share a picture of your doubt.
