Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What kind of experts work with Kutuki for developing engaging content?

Kutuki was started by Sneha Sundaram, an LSE alumna, classical singer, music educator and Bharath Bevinahally, an ISB alumni, guitarist and audio producer. The team comprises of award winning preschool educators, storytellers, artists and musicians who thoughtfully create every piece of content from scratch to make learning incredibly fun for pre-schoolers.

For what age group is Kutuki’s content suitable for?

The Kutuki's content is suitable for kids belonging to age group of 3-7 years.

What is the basis of Kutuki's curriculum content?

The content of Kutuki is based on a world class pre-school curriculum to ensure that child’s foundational skills grow stronger every day. It is very well researched and takes best practices from world class early learning methodologies including the Montessori methods, multiple intelligences, Reggio Emilia and the Waldorf method. Kutuki uses the creative and child friendly medium of songs and stories to teach a wide range of pre-school concepts including English language development, Early Math skills, Good Habits, Pre-Writing Skills, General Knowledge, Health and Nutrition, Science and much more in a fun, developmentally appropriate and contextual manner.

Kutuki’s content is suitable for which boards?

Kutuki’s content is suitable for Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) and State Level Boards.

What types of content are included in Kutuki course?

It has a wide range of learning resources which includes hundreds of engaging and animated stories, fun videos, rhymes, games, quizzes and interactivities to make the journey of little ones unstoppable.

What are the themes of Kutuki's content

All pre-school & early learning themes like Shapes, Colours, Numbers, Phonics, letter tracing, Festivals, General Knowledge, Good Habits, Moral Stories and Science are available.

In which languages Kutuki’s content are available?

Kutuki strongly believes that language should not be a barrier in learning and mother tongue can be used as a great source of strength to augment learning in little ones. While most of the content is in English, but it offer videos in Hindi, Tamil, Kannada and Marathi.

Is Kutuki safe in the world of internet?

Yes, it is 100% safe, as there advertisements doesn't appear during the use.

Is Kutuki developed in India?

Kutuki is 100% Indian. The context of each video, story, game or animation is taken from the social, cultural background of India as well as the accent of recording is also Indian.

Is each video, story or game mapped with learning outcome?

Yes, each video, story, game, activity or rhyme is well mapped with learning outcomes recommended by NCERT.

Is there a trial period of the App and What happens post trial period?

Yes, there is a trial period of 7 days and after the trial period content is blocked, if the subscription is not made.

What is the validity of the App?

After purchasing, the subscription is valid for 1 year from the date of purchase.

How one can start using the Kutuki App?

For using the Kutuki App follow the below mentioned simple steps:
Step 1. Download the App in Mobile of a parent.
Step 2. Use the mobile number of parent of the child to register.
Step 3. Use the OTP received on the mobile of the parent to verify.
Step 4. Fill the basic details required and choose the preferred language.
Step 5. Start watching the content of your choice.

What if a subscriber changes his/her mobile?

In case of the mobile change, subscriber should use the same mobile number with which the registration was done for the first time. The OTP (to login in a new device) is sent only to the registered mobile number.

Is it possible to use the same subscription in two devices at the same time?

No. A subscription can be used at a time in one device only.

Can the content be downloaded in the mobile/computer for later use?

No, the content cannot be downloaded in mobile/computer.

Does Kutuki work offline?

No, it does not work offline.

Can a child watch videos or stories in any sequence as per his/her choice?

Yes. A child is free to choose what he/she likes to watch. The content is not sequential in nature.
