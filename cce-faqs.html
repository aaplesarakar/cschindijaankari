Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE) is an Entrepreneurship course offered by CSC Academy & Indian School of Business, Hyderabad.

What is Indian School of Business (ISB)?

Indian School of Business (ISB) is a top ranked global B-school offering world class management education, ISB through its Srini Raju Centre for IT and Networked Economy (SRITNE), a multidisciplinary research centre esteemed at fostering rigorous and relevant research, education and outreach that advances our understanding of how Information and Communication Technologies (ICTs) create value for business and society. SRITNE was launched in July 2006.

What is the eligibility criterion for Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

Any general citizen, student, professional or housewife who intends to become an Entrepreneur can apply for the course.

Where can a candidate register for the Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

Candidate can register by logging in to http://cscentrepreneur.in/

How much is the fee for Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

The fee for the Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE) is Rs 1300/- + GST (non refundable).

What is the duration of the training program?

The duration of the training program is 40 hours.

What are the modules which are covered under the Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

Total 10 Modules are covered under Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE):
Module 1: Entrepreneurship
Module 2: Entrepreneurship and Entrepreneurial Character
Module 3: Identifying business opportunities
Module 4: Understanding Cost Structures
Module 5: Long term orientation
Module 6: Recording Business Transactions
Module 7: Basic Financial Terms
Module 8: Accounting and Business Reporting
Module 9: Marketing Education Handling Questions Concerns
Module 10: Marketing Education Value

Is it mandatory to clear all the Assessments to get certified?

Yes, it is mandatory to clear all the 10 Assessments to get certified under Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE).

How a candidate can get certificate under Certificate Course in Entrepreneurship (CCE)?

After clearing all the Assessments successfully, candidate can download the certificate online through TEC portal.

What are the system requirements for managing assessments?

System requirements for successfully managing assessments are:
• Operating system: Windows 10 with 4 GB RAM
• Integrated or an external Web cam is must
• Internet connectivity with 1 Mbps speed is required.

What is the format of questions in the online assessment process?

Questions in the assessments are in the form of multiple choice questions (MCQs).
