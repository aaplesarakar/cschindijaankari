Education

Home / Education Services / Diploma in Computer Applications

Process Flow

Step 1: Visit the CSC Digipaathshala website with a URL http://digipaathshala.cscacademy.org/

Step 2: Under the dashboard click on My Account and then further click on Centre Login

Step 3: Then click on Connect with Digital Seva option

Step 4: VLE will be directed to Digital Seva dashboard and login into the system by entering user id and password

Step 5: In the dashboard search for Digipaathshala Courses

Step 6: Then click on Add Student and fill all required details of the student. After submission of details, click on Submit option

Step 7: Now, student will be registered in the learning management system (LMS)

Step 8: Go to http://digipaathshala.cscacademy.org/ and browse the Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA) course

Step 9: Then click on Learn Now option and further click on Enroll Now option

Step 10: Login into the system and upload 10th grade certificate

Step 11: Then proceed for payment and after completion of payment, student can login into LMS and can start accessing the course
