Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

Who can register for this course?

Higher secondary school pass/fail students, graduates, working professionals, CA/CPT/CS appeared students, housewives/businessman, MBA/engineering students, or any candidate who is interested in learning Tally and wants to pursue it as career choice can opt for this course. Candidate from any background can register themselves for this course.

What is the Language of content?

Content of the course is in English language.

What are fees of various courses and VLE Commission?

Course Level Content Provided Course Fee VLE Commission
Complete Self Learning Manual on Financial Accounting with Basic GST Book 400 + GST Rs. 75 / -
Complete Self Learning Manual on Financial Accounting with Advanced GST Book 400 + GST Rs. 75 / -
Certificate Course in Financial Accounting with Basic GST Complete Kit 900 + GST Rs. 168.75 /-
Certificate Course in Financial Accounting with Advanced GST Complete Kit 900 + GST Rs. 168.75 /-

Can VLE change his/her Password?

Yes, VLEs can change their password from their Dashboard.

What are the user id and password candidates?

Candidates Credentials are:
User name: Candidates Mail ID (Filled at the time of registration)
Password: Candidates Mobile Number (Filled at the time of registration)

Can VLE change candidate’s details?

Yes candidate’s details can be changed. (Only Name, Mobile Number, E-Mail ID and Address)

What is the validity of the course?

Validity of the course is 2 months/60 days.

What is the Language of content?

Content of the course is in English language

What all is included in the course?

The course includes:

• E-book (Access from CSC Portal)
• Hard Copy of Book: A Complete Self Learning Manual on Tally ERP 9 with GST (Basics)
• Access to Mock (Practice) Test on CSC Portal
• Tally Educational software

What is the examination process?

Candidate can appear for the examination after one month of registration.

What if the candidate fails in his/her first attempt of examination?

Candidate will be given 2 attempts o clear the examination. On failing the examination first time, he/she can appear again after 15 days from the first examination attempt date.

What is the language of examination?

Examination is conducted in English language.

What is the format of questions in the examination?

Questions in the examination is in forma of multiple choice questions (MCQs)

What is duration of the examination?

Candidate will have one hour to finish their examination.

How many questions are there in the examination and what are the passing marks?

There will be total 50 questions, each carrying one mark. Students have to score minimum 16 marks or more to pass the examination.

Where will be the examination center?

VLEs CSC center will be the examination center.

How can the candidate check their results?

Candidate can see their result on their dashboard.

Certificate will be online or offline?

Digital verifiable certificate will be given to candidate on passing the examination and candidate can download the same from their dashboard.

Is there any grading system in the examination?

Yes, each Student will be given grades according to the marks they have obtained.

Marks Obtained Grade
20-16 "C" Grade
21-30 "B" Grade
31-40 "A" Grade
41-50 " A+" Grade

Does biometric device or camera is required for conducting the examination?

For examination no biometric device and camera is required.
