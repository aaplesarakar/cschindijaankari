Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

Who can register for this course?

Higher secondary school pass/fail students, graduates, working professionals, CA/CPT/CS appeared students, housewives/businessman, MBA/engineering students, or any candidate who is interested in learning Tally and wants to pursue it as career choice can opt for this course. Candidate from any background can register themselves for this course.

What is the Language of content?

Content of the course is in English language.

Will the course includes updated information of Tally?

Yes, the course includes latest version of Tally i.e. Tally ERP 9. Tally ERP 9 Software is one of the most financial accounting systems used in India. For small and medium enterprises, it is complete enterprise software.

What is the validity of the course?

Validity of the course is 2 months/60 days.

Can VLE change candidate’s details after registration?

No, VLE cannot change or alter any changes in candidate’s information.

What is the examination process?

Candidate can appear for the examination after one month of registration.

What if candidate fails in his/her first attempt?

Candidate will be given two attempts to clear the examination. On failing first time, he/she can appear again after 15 days of first examination date. No third attempt will be given to candidate.

What will be the language of examination?

Examination is conducted in English language.

What is the mode of examination? Examination is subjective or objective?

Tally examination will be objective and an online process, where candidates have to choose one correct answer from the given options.

Will there be any Mock test to prepare candidate for final Tally examination? subjective or objective?

Yes, candidates can appear for a Mock test. Candidates can appear for Mock test any number of times.

What will be the format for questions?

Questions in the examination will be in the format of Multiple Choice Questions.

What will be the duration of Tally Exam?

Candidate will have one hour to finish their examination.

Where will be the examination centre?

VLEs CSC centre will be the examination centre.

Where will be candidates result get uploaded?

Candidate can see their result on their dashboard.

When will candidate get the certificate?

Once candidate passes the examination, their certificate will be uploaded in 2 days on their dashboard.

Certificate will be online or offline?

Certificate will be in digital form. Candidate can download it from their dashboard.

What information does candidate need at the time of registration?

The student/candidate has to enter their name, contact details, location, and test centre in the registration fields.

Who will schedule the assessment for the candidate/student?

Assessments will be scheduled by the VLE, after one month of registration.

Where will the assessment be visible?

Assessment link will be visible in the student portal.

What is the fee structure and VLE commission?

Course Level Content Provided Assessment Fee VLE Commission
Tally Certification Program E-Course material Rs 1000/- + GST Rs. 160
