Will the Adda247 Online live classes be taken by the subject-expert?

Yes, Adda247 brings all the live classes from the subject experts having years of experience.

Will student be getting the recorded sessions for the live classes in case he/she miss any topic?

Yes, student can revise with the recorded sessions that will be provided to them.

What is the validity of recorded sessions?

The recorded sessions will be available till the validity of the pack purchased.

Will there be any extra charges for asking doubts from the teachers about the session?

No, there will not be any extra charges as the platform teachers are always available for clearing the doubts.

Are Adda247 SSC Online live classes available in any other language?

Yes, student can avail Adda247 SSC Online live classes in Hindi as well as English language.

Are online live Classes by Adda247 expensive?

No, the online live classes are available at an affordable price.

Does Adda247 provide online live classes for Defence examinations?

Yes, Addda247 provides Defence Online Live Classes for several Defence Exams such as CDS, AFCAT, and conquest CAPF AC, and so on.
