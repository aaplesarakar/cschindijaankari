Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

Courses of Symbiosis Center for Distance Learning (SCDL) are approved by any government body?

Yes, Management programs (refer program list on website) offered by SCDL are approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for AY 2021-22.

Can a student discuss/speak to admission counsellor or get personalized counselling?

Yes, a student can speak or chat online to an admission counsellor or get personalised counselling at SCDL Campus at Pune or via online counselling.

What are the eligibility criteria for Post Graduate Diploma programs?

The eligibility criteria for the two year and one year Post Graduate Diploma is, Bachelor’s degree holder or Graduates in any discipline from a recognized University. However, students are strongly recommended to check on the website i.e. https://www.scdl.net for the eligibility criteria in detail for the desired program.

What is provisional admission?

Provisional Admission is given, when admission related eligibility criteria is not fulfilled as per SCDL policies and documents are having deficiencies like: Coloured passport size recent photograph is not submitted or are not clear. Academic Qualification documents are not proper. Defence category proof: service certificate/Discharge book/Dependent certificate (if any) is not attached is not in prescribed format. Visa/Passport copy (for International/SAARC students) is not enclosed. Name change document copy (if any) is not enclosed. Application form is not signed/not submitted his signature. Submission of proper documents as per policies, within the timelines as specified by SCDL, for confirmation of admission, is the sole responsibility of the student.

What are the learning methods at SCDL?

Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning has a blended approach to student learning with a combination of - traditional and e-books (Program specific), e-learning and faculty interaction. Tutors use online chats and online classrooms to stimulate discussions. These sessions allow students to interact with faculty and clear their doubts immediately. `Symbiosis World Campus` - a powerful web portal, forms the centre of learning with facilities like - Assignment Submission, Computer-Based Examination, Message Boards and E-Mentoring etc.

Does SCDL offer placements on completion of the programmes?

Yes, SCDL offers placement assistance to the students.

What are the minimum system requirements for online mode of study?

System requirements for completion of online mode of study are:

• Minimum internet speed required is 512 Kbps or above.
• Internet browser: IE version 8.0 & above. OS requirement: Windows XP with Service Pack 3, Windows 7, Windows 8.1
• Flash player of version 10.0 or above
• Recommended 1 GB of RAM or above
• Processor type: Intel Dual Core compatible processor or above.
• If you have trouble joining the virtual class or to open the online
assignments, uninstall the existing flash & Mozilla Firefox from your desktop.

Are the programs offered at SCDL accepted by the industry?

Programs offered by Symbiosis have a very high industry acceptance. In addition, Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning is an education provider to leading global corporates such Airtel, Bajaj Finance Ltd., Bharti Pvt. Ltd., Cipla, Cognizant, Collabera, Eaton Technologies Pvt. Ltd., HCL, Hindalco Industries Ltd., IBM, Vodafone, Walmart, Wipro, TATA AIA, EFL Pvt. Ltd., SBI General Insurance, Arvind Ltd. EPIC Research, TAFE Ltd. etc. and many more.

What is the evaluation method for the program?

Each subject of all programs usually comprises of an assignment and computer based examination. The assignment is available in the student login and can be completed as per the respective semester schedule. The examinations include a combination of objective and subjective element. Examinations are conducted at designated exam centers across the country. Students are required to book their slots as per their time and pace.

Does the student have to pay for exam fees?

Yes, student is required to pay exam fees per exam per attempt separately. Exam fee is not a part of program fee.

When and where can a student appear for the exams?

Examinations are released in the 3rd month of each semester and the link for the same is available in the student login. Student can appear for the examinations at designated exam centers. Student can book their examination slot as per their time convenience. The exam booking can be done through the student login.

What if a student fails in exams? Can he/she retake their exams?

Yes student can retake the exam. The examination fees are applicable for the retake subjects.

When does results for examinations are declared?

The results of the exam are declared officially within 15 working days, from the date of exam to be appeared. A consolidate status of the results of the subjects is available in the student login for your reference.
