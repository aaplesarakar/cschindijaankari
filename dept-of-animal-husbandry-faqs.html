Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What are the various scheme of DoAHD?

Several schemes and programs that are governed by the DoAHD are:

• National Livestock Mission
• Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund
• Rashtriya Gokul Mission
• Kisan Credit Cards to Livestock Farmers
• National Programme for Dairy Development
• Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund
• Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations
• Livestock Health & Disease Control
• National Animal Disease Control Programme
• Livestock Census & Integrated Sample Survey

What is the target of proposed scheme?

The proposed scheme is focused and targeted towards:

• Entrepreneurship development
• Breed up gradation
• Productivity enhancement
• Feed and Fodder development
• Livestock Insurance
• Extension, Research and Innovation
• Employment generation

What is the role of state agencies and governments?

Role of state agencies and governments include:

• To scrutinize the applications received through portal and recommend the applications for availing by the entrepreneurs/eligible entities from the bank or financial institutions
• The state implementing agency shall be responsible for monitoring the projects of the entrepreneurs and state government. Then intimate DoAHD about their progress on half-yearly basis.

What is the VLE commission under the programme?

2120 Rs per camp will be given to VLE under the programme.

How many camps can be conducted per location?

VLE can organize one camp per location only.
