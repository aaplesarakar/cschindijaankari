Frequently Asked Questions - FAQs

What is Boston Training Academy Course?

Boston Training Academy (BTA) offers structured and highly customizable courses backed by world-class infrastructure, tools and techniques, and delivered by global experts. It mainly focus on to strive for excellence in the overall learning experience. Through collaboration with CSC Academy it provides certification in various fields as Data Science, Business Analytics, and Artificial Intelligence.

What is required eligibility for enrolling in the program?

Required eligibility for enrolling is:

i. For Certified Professional Program in Data Science
• Any Bachelors degree in sciences with Mathematics or programming language as a paper.
• Any B.E/B.Tech students and working professionals
• Basic working knowledge of Computers

ii. For Certified Professional Program in Data Science
• Any Bachelors degree in sciences with Mathematics as a core subject/optional subject with passion to learn online
• B.Sc, B.B.M, B.Com, M.Com, MBA
• Basic working knowledge of Computers

iii. For Certified Professional Program in Data Science
• Any Bachelors degree in sciences with Mathematics as a core subject with experience in programming
• B.Sc, M.Sc, B.E/B.Tech, M.E/M.Tech and Ph.D
• Basic working knowledge of Computers.

What are the expected job opportunities through the course?

Expected job opportunities available through the program are as:

  1. Data Scientist
  2. Business Analyst
  3. Assistant Manager- Analytics
  4. Data Analyst
  5. Artificial Intelligence Engineer
  6. Software Engineer For Artificial Intelligence And Data Science
  7. Machine Learning Engineer
  8. Statistical Analyst
  9. Statistical Modeler
  10. Data Mining Engineer
  11. Natural Language Processing (NLP) Engineer
  12. SQL Developer
  13. Deep Learning Engineer
  14. Computer Vision Engineer

What is the mode of learning?

All programs are delivered in Blended mode – mix of self-paced through online e-learning platform and Live sessions. This learning is supplemented by online practice using specific tools. Depending on the course candidate is required to go through all learning content, submit assignments and complete respective Quiz.

What all are included in the course?

Each course has five components – Online self-paced videos, live classes, Assignments or Practice, Project and quizzes for recapitulation, and assessment. Every live class will be followed by an assignment. Assignment may be given during live classes or students may be asked to do it on their own and submit/discuss in next live class.

What is the medium of instructions in the course?

The courses are available for students in two languages – English and Hindi.

What is the duration of the course?

The course duration is 2 months.

What is the assessment process for the course?

Assessment will be done through quizzes, assignments and end-course project.

What is the required qualifying percentage for obtaining course certification?

Qualifying percentage for attaining course certification is 40%.

Who are associated as faculty with the course?

The core learning content has been developed by Boston Training Academy. Faculty involved in delivering Live sessions are experts with 5-10 years of experience in the respective field.

What is the certification process?

After the successful completion of the course students will be able to download an e-certificate from their dashboard. To become a Certified Professional, individually 5 courses need to be successfully completed under these programs, only after that Candidate will be awarded by these Certifications. The certificate will be provided jointly by CSC Academy and BTA.
